Item 11.

Number of Americans killed by undocumented immigrants in \(2018\text{.}\)
  1. Note that no data exists.
  2. Given that there were \(15,000\) murders total, we need to estimate the number of undocumented immigrants in the US in \(2018\text{,}\) as a percentage of the population. Say between \(1\) and \(10\%\text{,}\) with geometric mean \(3\%\text{.}\) Very close to the real number!
  3. You may have heard the (true) claim that undocumented Americans commit crimes at a lower rate than documented Americans.
  4. Even if you assume the rate is the same, that means that \(3\%\) of the \(15,000\) murders, or
    \begin{equation*} .03(15000)=450\approx500 \end{equation*}
    murders were committed by undocumented immigrants in \(2018\text{.}\)
  5. Both the mass shooting and murder numbers are in the hundreds, so there’s absolutely no evidence for the “tens of thousands” claim made in the post.
