Item 10.

Average number of Americans who die each day due to mass shootings.
  1. How to define a “mass shooting”?
  2. No official definition: at least \(4\) killed? \(4\) injured? Including shooter or not? Including violence due to drugs/gangs?
  3. Estimate the number of mass shootings in \(2018\text{:}\) between \(10=1\times10^{1}\) and \(100=1\times10^{2}\text{.}\) Take the geometric mean: \(1\times10^{1.5}\approx30\text{.}\)
  4. Estimate the number of Americans killed in each mass shooting: say \(5\) on average. So a total of
    \begin{equation*} 5\times30=150 \end{equation*}
    Americans are estimated to have been killed by mass shootings in \(2018\text{.}\)
