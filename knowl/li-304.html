Item 8.

Average tax rate for an American teacher.
  1. The average teacher earns \(\$60,000\) a year. This means their tax rate is \(\$14,000\) plus \(25\%\) of the amount over \(\$80,000\text{.}\) Since there’s no amount over \(\$80,000\text{,}\) this teacher pays \(\$14,000\approx\$15,000\) in taxes.
  2. \(\$15,000\) is \(\frac{1}{4}\) or \(25\%\) of the teacher’s income of \(\$60,000\text{.}\)
